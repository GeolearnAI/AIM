Augmented Intelligent Minining

A change of paradigm in data assimilation and imaging for the mining industry

GeoLEARN comes with a change of paradigm in data assimilation and imaging for the mining industry. The Augmented Intelligent Mining (AIM) system will yield unprecedented integration of knowledge and unmatched levels of automation, enhancing exploration and exploitation management. The AIM platform will enable real-time integration of drillhole and geological data combined with augmented reality visualization on portable devices, keeping players updated and providing great value for raising capital along the way.
